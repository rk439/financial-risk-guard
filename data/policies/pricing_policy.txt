PRICING AND OVERAGE POLICY DOCUMENT
Document ID: POL-2026-X8

1. BASE SUBSCRIPTION DEFINITION
All standard corporate tier accounts include up to 5,000 GB of monthly cloud storage and 100,000 search queries per month.

2. OVERAGE AND ANOMALY CHARGES
Section 2.1: If a client exceeds the 5,000 GB storage threshold, additional consumption is billed dynamically at a flat rate of $0.10 per GB.
Section 2.2: Unexpected surges exceeding 200% of the baseline average usage within a 48-hour window are flagged as "Unmanaged Elastic Usage" and must be cleared by finance before contract renewal.
